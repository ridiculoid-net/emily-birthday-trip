01
Isla Mujeres, Mexico
Tiny car-free island · 20-min ferry from Cancún
Best WalkabilityMost AffordableHurricane season
The BeachPlaya Norte — consistently one of the top beaches in the Caribbean. Waist-deep turquoise water, dead calm, almost no waves. Soft white sand.
Water Clarity⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — electric turquoise, pristine visibility
Walkability to Food
The entire island is 5 miles long. Golf carts are the main transport. The main town center has excellent tacos, seafood, grocery stores, and beach bars all within a 5–10 min walk.
How Touristy?Day-trippers from Cancún arrive by 11am and leave by 4pm. Stay past sunset and it feels like a local Mexican island.
Getting ThereCHS → CUN (direct on Breeze, Spirit, or 1-stop via ATL/CLT)
Then: Ultramar Ferry from Puerto Juárez, ~20 min, $8–15 each way. Passport needed.
August WeatherWarm (~88°F), partly cloudy, occasional afternoon showers. Still beautiful beach weather.
Wildcard PerkSwim with whale sharks in late July–August — one of the most bucket-list experiences in the Caribbean.
CHS→CUN+ 20-min ferry
Breeze, Spirit, American w/ 1 stop · ~3h flight
~$300–500 pp round-trip from CHS
MIA → CUN often $200–$350 pp RT

02
St. John, USVI
No passport needed · 15-min ferry from St. Thomas
Least TouristyUS TerritoryHurricane season
The BeachTrunk Bay (UNESCO snorkeling trail), Cinnamon Bay, Hawksnest Bay — some of the most pristine beaches in the entire Caribbean. Two-thirds of the island is US National Park.
Water Clarity⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — exceptional snorkeling, crystal visibility, sea turtles common
Walkability to Food
Cruz Bay is a walkable hub with grocery stores (Starfish Market), excellent restaurants, and bars.
How Touristy?No large chain hotels. No vendors on beaches. No high-rises. True low-key island life.
Getting ThereCHS → STT (1 stop via CLT, MIA, or ATL)
Then: Red Hook Ferry to Cruz Bay, ~15 min, ~$15 each way
No passport needed — US territory ✓
August Weather~87°F, humid, some rain possible. Peak hurricane risk Aug–Sept. Buy travel insurance.
Wildcard PerkRenting a Jeep ($75–$100/day) to explore hidden beaches. Lameshur Bay and Salt Pond Bay are stunning and nearly empty.
CHS→STT+ 15-min ferry
American, Delta, United · 1 stop via CLT / MIA / ATL
~$400–600 pp round-trip from CHS
More direct options from MIA — worth checking

03
Aruba — Eagle Beach
Outside the hurricane belt · Consistently sunny · ABC Islands
Safest for AugustNo hurricane risk ✓More touristy
The BeachEagle Beach is consistently ranked among the best in the Caribbean — wider and far less crowded than Palm Beach. Calm, flat water on the leeward (west) coast.
Water Clarity⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Very clear and calm. Beautiful and swimmable every single day.
Walkability to Food
Eagle Beach strip has a handful of good restaurants. Palm Beach (5 min drive) has every restaurant type imaginable.
How Touristy?Eagle Beach is quieter than Palm Beach. Honestly a tourist island — but the local side (San Nicolas, Savaneta) has authentic flavor.
Getting ThereCHS → AUA (1 stop via MIA, CLT, or JFK)
Direct flights from MIA to AUA (~3.5h). Passport required.
August Weather⭐ Best August weather of any option. Outside the hurricane belt. Consistent trade winds keep it cooler (~84°F). Rarely rains. ~340 sunny days/year.
Wildcard PerkSnorkeling the Antilla shipwreck (largest in the Caribbean). Natural pool in Arikok National Park. Baby Beach on the south tip.
CHS→AUA
American, Delta, United · 1 stop via MIA or CLT
~$450–600 pp round-trip from CHS
American has frequent direct MIA → AUA flights (~3.5h)

04
Turks & Caicos
Grace Bay · Providenciales · #1 beach in the world, year after year
Best Water QualityStretch budgetHurricane season
The BeachGrace Bay Beach — 12-mile stretch of powder-white sand and the clearest, most vivid turquoise water in the world. Protected by a barrier reef, always calm.
Water Clarity⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — arguably the best in the Caribbean. The barrier reef keeps it flat, clear, and perfect every day.
Walkability to Food
The Grace Bay strip has Mango Reef, Shark Bite, Coco Van food truck, restaurants and shops — walkable from most accommodations.
How Touristy?More developed than the others. Upscale resort vibe — think luxury travelers. Still not overcrowded like Cancún.
Getting ThereCHS → PLS (1 stop via CLT or MIA — American, Delta)
~5.5h total travel time. Passport required.
August Weather~87°F, occasionally stormy. Peak of hurricane season — mid-August is statistically one of the riskier weeks. Buy travel insurance.
Budget Note$300/night will be tight. The Grace Bay Studio is the standout budget pick at 5.0★ with 86 reviews.

American, Delta · 1 stop via CLT or MIA · ~5.5h total
~$450–600 pp round-trip from CHS
